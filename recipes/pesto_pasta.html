Delicious Chicken Pesto Pasta

This recipe works well as a stand-alone meal, or as a complementary side-dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts cubed into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 Package of preferred pasta (Rotini recommended)
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • Crushed red pepper (optional - add to taste)
  • 1/3 cup Sun-dried Tomatoes
  • 1 cup diced fresh tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced black olives (optional)
  • 1 jar or about 1/2 cup pesto sauce (store bought is fine)
  • Salt and/or preferred seasonings

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta until tender
  2. Cook chicken and cut into cubes or strips
  3. Add chicken to pasta
  4. Add Pesto and mix well
  5. Add tomoatoes and Olives
  6. Season with salt and/or other preferred seasoning
